About Daiva Anughara

A sanctuary for authentic spiritual transmission

🕉️

Our Sacred Mission

Daiva Anughara is conceived as a spiritual sanctuary dedicated to preserving, sharing, and facilitating the practice of ancient wisdom, with a profound focus on the sacred tradition of Bhairava Sadhana and Sādhanā Paddhati.

Our sole intent is to make authentic spiritual knowledge accessible to sincere seekers. We believe true spiritual wisdom is a transmission of divine grace, meant to be shared freely from the heart, stripped of commercial transactions, yet preserved with the utmost reverence and purity it demands.

From the Heart

I am Manjunath Mahendrakar, a humble seeker walking the timeless path of Maa Kamakhya Bhairava tattva. Guided unconditionally by the blessings of my Diksha Guru, the Avadhutas, and the boundless grace of the Siddhars, I act merely as a vessel.

What is shared here are not mere teachings, but sacred glimpses—tiny, luminous ripples from the vast ocean of His consciousness. This offering is a living prayer to Guru Bhairava; a space born entirely from love, devotion, and reverence for the fierce compassion of our Iṣṭa Devatā and the hidden, mystical treasures of the Puranas.

Our Guiding Principles

Devotion (Bhakti)

Approaching all practices with deep reverence, recognizing the divinity in the knowledge we share.

Selfless Service (Seva)

Serving humanity without expectation of reward, embodying the spirit of pure selflessness.

Authentic Lineage

Committed to traditional, unaltered knowledge passed down through legitimate spiritual Gurus.

Free Offering

All sacred guidance is offered freely, as an unconditioned transmission of divine grace.

Prāṇi Daya Sanga

"The Assembly of Compassion for All Beings"

Prāṇi Daya Sanga is the beating heart of our charitable endeavors. It reflects our unyielding commitment to extending unconditional love, care, and protection to every living creature around us.

Animal Welfare

Providing nourishment, care, and safe haven for animals in need.

Children's Education

Supporting the educational journey of children from Blind Schools and special needs institutions.

Community Upliftment

Extending a helping hand to foster well-being and harmony in society.

Seva Opportunity: If your heart feels moved to support these initiatives, you may offer seva. This is purely voluntary—a silent offering from soul to soul. There are no tax benefits or formal receipts, only grace.
